# Supermarket Billing Software — User Guide

## Logging in

Open the website address given by your admin. Enter your username and password and click
"Sign in."

If you forgot your password, ask your admin to reset it from **Staff Accounts**.

---

## Dashboard

The first screen you see after logging in. It shows:
- **Today's Sales** — total money taken in today
- **Total Products** — how many items are in your catalog
- **Low Stock Alerts** — items running low and needing reorder
- **Total Customers** — how many customers are registered
- A list of products that need restocking
- Your most recent bills

---

## Making a sale (Billing / POS)

1. Click **Billing / POS** in the left menu.
2. Type a product name in the search box, or scan a barcode with a barcode scanner — it
   will be added automatically when scanned.
3. Click a search result to add it to the cart on the right.
4. To change quantity, use the **+** / **−** buttons next to each item in the cart.
5. (Optional) Click **Add customer** to attach the sale to a customer record — this also
   builds their purchase history and loyalty points.
6. (Optional) Enter a discount percentage.
7. Choose the payment method (Cash, Card, UPI, Other).
8. Click **Generate Bill**. You'll be taken to the printable invoice.
9. Click **Print Bill** to print — choose **A4 / Letter** or **Thermal (80mm)** from the
   dropdown depending on your printer before printing.

Stock is deducted automatically the moment a bill is generated — no manual step needed.

---

## Managing products

Go to **Products**.

- **Add Product**: click the button, fill in name, price, and opening stock quantity, then
  Save. Barcode is optional but recommended for fast billing.
- **Edit**: click the pencil icon on any row to change its name, price, or tax rate.
- **Adjust stock**: click the stock icon to record a delivery (Stock In), damage/loss
  (Stock Out), or to set an exact count (e.g. after a physical stock count).
- **Remove**: click the trash icon. This hides the product from new bills but keeps it in
  old invoices for your records.

---

## Managing customers

Go to **Customers**.

- **Add Customer**: name is required; phone/email/address are optional.
- Click the eye icon to see a customer's full purchase history.
- Loyalty points build up automatically (1 point per 100 currency spent) — shown in the
  Loyalty Points column.

---

## Inventory

Go to **Inventory** to see:
- A live list of everything currently low on stock.
- A full log of every stock change — deliveries, damages, manual adjustments, and sales —
  with who made the change and when.

---

## Reports

Go to **Reports**.

- Switch between **Today**, **Last 7 Days**, or **Last 30 Days**.
- See total revenue, number of bills, tax collected, and (Admin only) profit.
- See your best-selling products and how customers are paying (cash/card/UPI).
- Click **Excel** or **PDF** to download the report for that period.

---

## Settings (Admin only)

Go to **Settings** to set:
- Your store name, address, and phone (shown on every printed bill)
- Currency symbol
- Default tax % applied to new products
- Invoice number prefix (e.g. "INV" → bills become INV-20260621-0001)

You can also change your own password here.

---

## Staff Accounts (Admin only)

Go to **Staff Accounts** to add new cashier logins. Choose role:
- **Staff** — can bill, manage products/customers/inventory, view non-financial reports.
- **Admin** — full access including settings and profit reports.

You can deactivate an account at any time without deleting its history.
